Founder of **[Prop Firm Deal Finder (PFDF)](https://propfirmdealfinder.com)** — the largest aggregator of proprietary trading firm discounts, reviews, and affiliate deals. Also building quiet tooling at the intersection of trading and AI under the **Koji** name.

## 🔥 What I'm building

### [Prop Firm Deal Finder](https://propfirmdealfinder.com)
A free platform that tracks live discount codes, challenge prices, reviews, and affiliate deals across 20+ prop trading firms (futures, forex, multi-asset). Available on iOS, Android, Microsoft Store, Chrome, and the web.

- 🌐 **Web:** [propfirmdealfinder.com](https://propfirmdealfinder.com)
- 📱 **iOS:** [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/prop-firm-deal-finder/id6749067413)
- 🪟 **Microsoft:** [Microsoft Store](https://apps.microsoft.com/store/detail/9P5HBF2CKQD3)
- 🧩 **Chrome Extension:** [Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/prop-firm-deal-finder/bhciijhpmnildmgnjbbbmdhhdebfhpfa)

### [propfirmdealfinder-mcp-server](https://github.com/chrisbusbin-pixel/propfirmdealfinder-mcp-server) — [![npm version](https://img.shields.io/npm/v/propfirmdealfinder-mcp-server.svg)](https://www.npmjs.com/package/propfirmdealfinder-mcp-server)
Model Context Protocol (MCP) server that lets Claude, ChatGPT, and any MCP-compatible AI query live prop firm discount codes, compare firms, and find the cheapest challenges. Published on the [Official MCP Registry](https://registry.modelcontextprotocol.io), Smithery, mcp.so, and more.

```bash
npx propfirmdealfinder-mcp-server
```
